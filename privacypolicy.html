West News App Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2023-06-06

This Privacy Policy describes how the West News app, developed and maintained by Alex Westerlund, collects and uses information from its users.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information about you. In other words, we do not collect information such as your name, address, phone number, or email address. However, we do collect anonymous, non-personally identifiable information, which is used to improve our app and provide a better user experience. This information includes:

  • Demographic information such as age, gender, and ethnicity.
  • Usage statistics, including frequency of use, alarm time and mode, and frequently accessed menus.

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of understanding our user base and optimizing our service. We use demographic data to understand our audience composition. This helps us measure the diversity of our audience, a key metric for a news organization in a well functioning democracy. We use usage data to, for example, identify popular features, improve user experience, and explore potential areas for improvement.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or disclose any of the anonymous, non-personally identifiable information we collect, as described above. All collected data is kept within our organization and used exclusively by us.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through the app.

6. Consent

By using West News, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
