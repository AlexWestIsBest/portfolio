About Me

I went to college for Philosophy. Think the BIG questions- who is a self-drivign car responsibile for when it approaches and unnavoidable crash? Who owns the copyright to AI-created content? These questions and more are what fascinated me with the larger world and the role we play within it.


I grew up outside Columbus, Ohio, the most famous test city in America. A combination of good diversity, political representation on both sides, and solid infrastructure have made Columbus a rare exception in a time of urban stagnation.

When you put it all together, you get someone who understands change. Growing up in a dynamic environment (the suburbs I was raised in were the fastest growing in America for a few years) has made me more comfortable than most in excelling in a fast-paced environment. While in school, I studied abroad 3 times! In the 4 years since I've graduated, I've launched startups, hosted a personal growth conference, met with billionaires to tackle sea level rise, and ran an international humanitarian organization.
